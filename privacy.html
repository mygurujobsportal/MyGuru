Privacy Policy (గోప్యతా విధానం)

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to MyGuru. We are committed to protecting your personal identity and data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect (మేము సేకరించే సమాచారం)

To provide a secure and verifiable recruitment network, we collect the following data:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full Name, Mobile Number, and Verification IDs (PAN or Academic ID).
  • Academic & Work Data: Hall ticket numbers, marks obtained, certificates details, and previous employment records.
  • Location Data: Area PIN Codes and device GPS coordinates (optional) to map local tuition/job availability.
  • Passport Photograph: Uploaded by teachers to verify physical profile authenticity to employers and parents.

2. How We Protect Your Data (డేటా భద్రత మరియు ప్రైవసీ)

  • Identity Safeguard: Mobile numbers, Gmail addresses, and social handles are securely masked and fully blurred on the public feed to protect user privacy from data scrapers or unauthorized callers.
  • Secure Storage: All your sensitive academic records, documents, and passport photographs are saved inside encrypted cloud databases managed securely.
  • In-App Chat Privacy: Secure communications handled natively inside the app are private and strictly protected from outside access.

3. Dynamic Service Policy Notice (సర్వీస్ పాలసీ మార్పుల నోటీసు)

MyGuru operates on a flexible, evolving digital framework. To improve safety or system efficiency, we reserve the right to alter features at any time. For example, while contact numbers are hidden currently in favor of secure chat, we may switch to a direct visual contact system later, or vice versa, depending on administrative updates.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information, credentials, or passport photographs to third-party marketing companies. Data is only utilized inside the MyGuru web eco-system to facilitate matches between recruiters and educators.
